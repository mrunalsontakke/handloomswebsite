HANDLOOMS IN INDIA

Handloom products in India are finely woven texture, where the exchange of structure and example, theme, weaving and painting or other compatible changes and blends, makes every item novel, everything as particular as the following. The diversity and variation of weaving techniques styles and designs have innumerable art forms and school of practice which make it a fairly Complex industry. We bring together an in-depth understanding and comprehensive overview of each of the platforms and leverage our expertise to cater to the precise needs and requirements of the clients. This makes the ecosystem fairly Complex and challenging for someone who wishes to manufacture or procure the right kind of textile at the right price. We bring to the table end-to-end expertise across the entire Handloom value chain, thereby offering a simplified interface between our clients and the vast population of Weavers and artisans.
